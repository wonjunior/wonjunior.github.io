About

I am a first year PhD student in the computer vision group of ASTRA (previously RITS), a joint team between Inria, Valeo.ai, and Valeo.

My research interests are on multi-task learning, domain adaptation and vision in adverse weather conditions. I am directed by Raoul de Charette.

I received a MSc degree in Artificial Intelligence from Telecom SudParis while in an exchange program at Telecom Paris.
